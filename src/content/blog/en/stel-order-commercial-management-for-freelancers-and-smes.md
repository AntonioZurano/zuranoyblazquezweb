If you still run your business with spreadsheets, scattered documents and paper
invoices, you are probably losing more time than you realise. **STEL Order** is
management software designed so freelancers and SMEs can keep everything under
control in one place.

## Your entire business in one tool

With STEL Order you can:

- Create **quotes, orders and delivery notes** and turn them into invoices with
  one click.
- Manage **customers** and sales follow-up.
- Control your **product catalogue**, pricing and stock.
- Record **purchases and expenses** to understand your real profitability.
- Organise your team's **diary and tasks**.

## Work from wherever you are

One of its greatest strengths is **mobility**: you can issue an invoice or check
a customer from your phone or tablet, with everything syncing instantly. Ideal
if you work on the road or visit clients in person.

## The key: a proper implementation

A powerful tool only delivers results when it is **properly configured** and
adapted to how you work. Installing it is not enough — you need to set up your
invoice series, import your data and train your team.
