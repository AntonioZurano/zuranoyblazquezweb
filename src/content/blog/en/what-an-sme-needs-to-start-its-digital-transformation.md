Digitising a business is not about buying lots of technology. It is about
**solving real problems** with the right tools. For an SME or freelancer, the
first steps can feel overwhelming, but with a simple plan you can see meaningful
improvements very quickly.

## Start with the essentials

Before thinking about large projects, make sure the basics are covered:

- **Your own domain** that represents your business.
- **Professional email** on your domain (no free consumer accounts).
- **A simple website** that explains what you do and how to contact you.
- **Backups** of your most important information.

With these four elements you already project a professional image and protect
your business from unexpected setbacks.

## Organise your day-to-day work

The next step is to **organise information and workflows**. This is where tools
like [Microsoft 365](/en/microsoft-365/) (email, documents and teamwork) and
management software such as [STEL Order](/en/stel-order/) come in — helping you
control quotes, invoices and customers without wasting time on repetitive tasks.

## Common mistakes to avoid

1. Subscribing to many tools without a clear goal.
2. Not training your team on what you implement.
3. Leaving security and backups for "later".
4. Having no one to call when something goes wrong.

## Conclusion

Digital transformation is a journey, not a leap. Start with the essentials,
organise your day-to-day work and rely on a technology partner who supports you.
